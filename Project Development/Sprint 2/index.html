Liver Disease Prediction

Liver disorders prevent the liver from functioning normally. Liver disease develops primarily as a result of heavy alcohol use. An effective task that can assist clinicians in quickly diagnosing the disease is the early prediction of liver disease using classification algorithms. It is a challenging effort for the doctors to identify liver illness at an early stage. The major goal of this project is to compare the predicted accuracy of several classification algorithms and examine their parameters in order to determine which one is the best at identifying liver illness.
